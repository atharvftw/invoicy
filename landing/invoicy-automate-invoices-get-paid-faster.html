FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still have something on your mind? Drop us a line.

Can I send WhatsApp reminders?
Yes. Invoicy generates 1-click WhatsApp share links with a pre-filled, polite reminder message and the invoice attached as a secure PDF link. Your client taps once and sees everything they need.
Is it safe for my client data?
We use bank-level AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, and audited access controls. Your data is never sold, never shared, and you can export everything at any time. We're built on India-region infrastructure for low latency and data residency.
Can I add GST details?
Yes — Invoicy fully supports Indian tax formats including CGST, SGST, IGST, HSN/SAC codes, place of supply, and reverse charge invoices. We handle interstate vs intrastate logic automatically based on the GSTIN you enter.
Do reminders feel automated and pushy?
No — that was the whole point. Our default templates are written like a human would write them: polite, professional, and appropriately firm only when an invoice is significantly overdue. You can fully customize the wording, or write your own.
Can I set up recurring invoices?
Recurring billing is available on the Pro plan. Set the cadence (weekly, monthly, quarterly), add an optional end date, and Invoicy generates and sends each invoice on schedule. Perfect for retainer clients.
What happens to my invoices if I cancel?
You can export every invoice as PDF or CSV at any time, even after canceling — including a full audit trail. Your data is yours. We hold onto your account for 90 days after cancellation in case you change your mind.
